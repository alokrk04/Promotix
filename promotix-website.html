FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Promotix different from other agencies?
+
Promotix combines premium creative production with data-driven strategy. We don't just create content — we craft campaigns that drive measurable results. Our local expertise in Karnataka paired with global creative standards gives clients the best of both worlds.
How long does it take to see results?
+
Most clients see significant improvements in brand visibility within 30–60 days. For organic growth and SEO, expect meaningful results within 3–6 months. Paid campaigns can drive immediate results from day one of launch.
Do you work with businesses outside Nipani/Belagavi?
+
Absolutely! We're based in Nipani but work with clients across India. All projects are managed remotely with regular video calls and full transparency through our project management system.
Can I upgrade or change my plan anytime?
+
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or customize your plan at any time. We offer flexible arrangements because we understand that business needs evolve. Just reach out to your account manager.
